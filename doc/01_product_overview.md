\# TradeAudit Product Overview



\## What is TradeAudit

TradeAudit is an AI trading system and investor account audit platform.



\## Core Purpose

Help traders audit strategies and real account performance before moving to the next stage.



\## Positioning

TradeAudit is not:

\- a signal-selling platform

\- an auto-trading promise platform

\- a guaranteed profit platform



TradeAudit is:

\- a strategy audit platform

\- an investor account audit platform

\- a forward and gate validation platform



\## V1 Focus

\- Dashboard

\- Strategy Lab

\- Backtest Center

\- Forward / Gate Report

\- Investor Account Audit

\- Calendar



\## Core Value

TradeAudit helps users:

\- review strategy quality

\- analyze real trading records

\- check forward readiness

\- make gate decisions

\- reduce costly mistakes before demo or live
