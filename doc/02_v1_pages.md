\# TradeAudit V1 Pages



\## 1. Dashboard

Purpose:

\- show overall project/account status



Blocks:

\- latest audit summary

\- latest backtest summary

\- latest forward summary

\- gate status

\- risk score

\- mini economic calendar



\## 2. Strategy Lab

Purpose:

\- manage strategies and versions



Blocks:

\- strategy list

\- parameter versions

\- champion flag

\- run backtest button

\- promote to forward button



\## 3. Backtest Center

Purpose:

\- review historical strategy performance



Blocks:

\- backtest parameters

\- summary metrics

\- profit factor

\- max drawdown

\- win rate

\- trade list

\- AI explanation summary



\## 4. Forward / Gate Report

Purpose:

\- compare forward performance and decide next-stage readiness



Blocks:

\- forward results

\- backtest vs forward comparison

\- gate decision

\- rejection reasons

\- next-step suggestions



\## 5. Investor Account Audit

Purpose:

\- analyze real MT5 investor account trading history



Blocks:

\- account connection

\- date range selection

\- trade history summary

\- risk metrics

\- behavior analysis

\- audit report output



\## 6. Calendar

Purpose:

\- improve retention and daily revisit rate



Blocks:

\- mini calendar on dashboard

\- full calendar page

\- high impact filter

\- currency filter

\- today / this week view
